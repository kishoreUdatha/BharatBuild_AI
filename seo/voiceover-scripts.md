# üìù QUICK TIPS FOR RECORDING

## Voice Tips
- Speak clearly and at medium pace
- Add energy and enthusiasm
- Pause briefly between sections
- Smile while speaking (it shows in your voice!)

## Recording Tips
- Use a quiet room
- Keep mic 6-8 inches from mouth
- Do 2-3 takes of each section
- Record room tone (10 sec silence) for editing

## Timing Tips
- Practice reading aloud first
- Time yourself with a stopwatch
- Adjust script if too long/short
- Leave space for visual moments

## Equipment (Budget)
- **Free**: Phone earbuds mic
- **Budget**: Boya BYM1 (‚Çπ500)
- **Better**: Maono AU-A04 (‚Çπ2000)
- **Best**: Blue Yeti (‚Çπ8000)
